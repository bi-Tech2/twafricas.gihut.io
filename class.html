Master Class: Empowering Entrepreneurs

Our exclusive Master Class is designed for aspiring and established entrepreneurs who are eager to build scalable businesses. Whether you're starting out or looking to take your business to the next level, this intensive training offers a comprehensive curriculum that touches on essential aspects of modern entrepreneurship.

What You Will Learn:

  • Business Strategy Development: Understand how to create a sustainable and competitive business strategy.
  • Market Research & Analysis: Learn how to research your market, understand your target audience, and analyze competitors effectively.
  • Digital Marketing: Gain hands-on skills in leveraging digital tools and platforms to grow your business online.
  • Financial Management: Learn how to handle your finances, manage cash flow, and secure funding for your business.
  • Innovation & Creativity: Unlock creative problem-solving skills to develop new products or services and stay ahead of the competition.
  • Leadership & Team Building: Develop leadership skills and learn how to build a motivated, effective team.
  • Networking & Collaboration: Build relationships with industry leaders and like-minded entrepreneurs to grow your business network.

Master Class Details:

Duration: 4 Weeks (Part-Time)

Mode of Learning: Online and In-Person (Hybrid Model)

Price: NGN 200,000 (Payment plans available)

Enrollment: Limited slots available. Early registration is encouraged.
