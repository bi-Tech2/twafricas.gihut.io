Our Commitment

Our Mission

To provide access to knowledge, funding, tools and market to help MSMEs grow. We are here to help them think big, start small and prosper.

Our Vision

To grow the Africa economy by creating an enabling ecosystem of prosperity for the young business person.

Our Mandates

The Network Africa summit has several mandates aimed at promoting and developing small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in Africa . Policy Formulation and Advocacy: we design and execute policies and strategies that create an enabling environment for MSME growth and development, including regulatory reforms. Capacity Building: we provide training, skill development, and capacity-building programs help prepare business owners for growth. Access to Finance: we facilitate, syndicate and implement specialised funding schemes for MSMEs (including low-interest loans and grants). Market Access: we enable MSMEs access local and international markets through market linkages, trade fairs, exhibitions, and other promotional activities. Research and Development: we conduct research and development activities to generate data and information relevant to SME development, which informs policy formulation and program implementation. Entrepreneurship Promotion: we promote entrepreneurship culture and encourage the establishment and growth of MSMEs across various sectors of the economy. Coordination and Collaboration: we coordinate with other government agencies, development partners, and stakeholders involved in SME development to ensure synergy and effective implementation of programs. Monitoring and Evaluation: we monitor and evaluate the impact of its programs and interventions on SMEs, to assess effectiveness and identify areas for improvement.
