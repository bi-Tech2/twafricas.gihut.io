The Network Africa Summit

The Network Africa Summit is Nigeria’s leading conference for national development and idea generation. It unites businesses, funders, young entrepreneurs, and key stakeholders to address urgent challenges and drive impact through youth entrepreneurship.

  • A gathering of Enterprise Support Organisations (ESOs) and Businesses

Our Commitment

Our Mission

To provide access to knowledge, funding, tools, and market to help MSMEs grow. We are here to help them think big, start small, and prosper.

Our Vision

To grow the Africa economy by creating an enabling ecosystem of prosperity for the young business person.

Our Mandates

The Network Africa Summit has several mandates aimed at promoting and developing small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in Africa.

Policy Formulation and Advocacy
We design and execute policies and strategies that create an enabling environment for MSME growth and development, including regulatory reforms.

Capacity Building
We provide training, skill development, and capacity-building programs to help prepare business owners for growth.

Access to Finance
We facilitate, syndicate, and implement specialized funding schemes for MSMEs (including low-interest loans and grants).

Market Access
We enable MSMEs to access local and international markets through market linkages, trade fairs, exhibitions, and other promotional activities.

Research and Development:
We conduct research and development activities to generate data and information relevant to SME development, which informs policy formulation and program implementation.

Entrepreneurship Promotion
We promote an entrepreneurship culture and encourage the establishment and growth of MSMEs across various sectors of the economy.

Coordination and Collaboration
We coordinate with other government agencies, development partners, and stakeholders involved in SME development to ensure synergy and effective implementation of programs.

Monitoring and Evaluation:
We monitor and evaluate the impact of our programs and interventions on SMEs to assess effectiveness and identify areas for improvement.
